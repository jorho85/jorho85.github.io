MAT 152 STATISTICAL METHODS ONE

FIRST TAUGHT Summer, 2023

In this course we look at data analysis and help students develop tools and skills to analize data and report their findings. This course will help students to understand the proper methods to collect the data, employ the correct analyses, and effectively present the results. Statistics is a crucial process behind how we make discoveries in science, make decisions based on data, and make predictions.

MAT 167 DESCRETE MATH

FIRST TAUGHTTBA

The word "discrete" means individually separate and distinct. Thus, when we study functions in discrete mathematics, their domain is often the natural numbers or some other subset of the integers - maybe, even a finite set. This course will cover many important topics that are essential for those seeking a career in the computer science field such as algorithms, number representation, logic, set theory, Boolean algebra, functions, combinatorics, algorithmic complexity, graph theory and trees.
